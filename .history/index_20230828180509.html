Greenthumb, hailing from the diverse and ever-evolving music scene of Canada, has carved a unique niche for himself in the world of underground hip-hop. Renowned for producing gritty, raw tracks that resonate with the soul of Phonk and Boombap, he encapsulates the very essence of what it means to be an underground artist. His beats, laced with deep basslines and haunting melodies, provide the perfect backdrop for his powerful lyricism, drawing listeners into a world that's both real and raw. Greenthumb continues to push boundaries, proving that hip-hop is as much about authenticity as it is about rhythm and rhyme. His evocative tracks are available on all major streaming platforms, allowing fans from around the world to delve into his unique sound.
